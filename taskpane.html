Job Search Functionality

The job search feature offers a range of options to refine your queries. Simply enter the relevant criteria to find the desired job listings.

You can conduct job searches using the following parameters:

  • UJID
  • Client Name
  • Location
  • Line Number
  • Operator
  • Product
  • Status (Not Started, In Progress, Complete)
  • Print Only/Mailing

The search bar is compatible with scan guns. When a barcode is scanned to initiate a job, the search bar will automatically clear, facilitating seamless re-entry of new queries.
